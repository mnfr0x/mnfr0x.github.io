Harvesting

This feature resembles royalties in that it deducts a percentage of the sale, but instead of being retained, it is sent to the address of the nft contract.

The contract governing these collections is structured to randomly allocate received Ethereum to one of its items, each item within these collections functions as a wallet capable of storing Ethereum, enabling withdrawal by its owner.
